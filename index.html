Björn Ekström

I am Lecturer at the Swedish School of Library and Information Science, University of Borås, Sweden.Here is my staff page. I explore human and nonhuman engagement with information in scholarly conduct, in scholars’ relations with nonscholars and/or in/with environmental settings. I am very interested in situated engagement with information and how this engagement is relationally tied to large-scale phenomena. I am particularly interested in sociomaterial practice theory and tend to work with qualitative and digital methods.See a list of publications at ORCiD or Google Scholar. I mainly teach courses related to information search and knowledge organisation.
