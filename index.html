Björn Ekström

PhD Candidate and Lecturer in Library and Information Science at the University of Borås, Sweden. My research interests include information practices, scholarly communication and digital methods. I am currently particularly interested in information practices in citizen science projects.

I am a member of University of Borås’s research groups Information Practices and Digital Cultures and Knowledge Infrastructures.
